Another Blog Yay

Written by Sarah Newman

September 25

Here is another blog entry where I need to ramble to get the word count. Today I went to my MI420 class. I finished my 3D print model for our third project. The assignment is where I have to print something to put on a little wind up walking toy. I made a couple different ideas but many people liked the sketch of the piranha plant from mario so I went with that. Making it in tinkercad took hours but it was worth it. Hopefully it will print well because it might need a lot of structure. I am printing it in resin so hopefully that helps and doesn't cost me a fortune. It needs to stop raining. I liked a little rain here or there but now there is too much moisture everywhere. I can't ride outside when it is pouring rain and the outdoor ring takes forever to dry from flooding. The horses are muddy and wet and their feet are going to get too moist if this rain keeps up. The horses don't care, they're horses, but I care. I am very close to the word count now. I am more likely to get run over by a scooter on campus than a car.
